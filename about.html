Our Story

Where heritage meets contemporary luxury

Redefining Luxury

The GlamourCart philosophy

Founded in Sandton in 2010, GlamourCart began as a vision to curate the world's most exquisite luxury goods in one unparalleled destination. What started as a boutique serving discerning clients has grown into a global platform redefining luxury ecommerce.

Our mission is simple yet profound: to connect connoisseurs with exceptional craftsmanship, timeless design, and the rarest materials through an experience that exceeds expectations at every touchpoint.

Today, we partner with over 200 master artisans and heritage houses across Africa, Europe, Asia, and the Americas to bring you a carefully edited selection of jewelry, timepieces, and luxury accessories.

Isabelle Laurent, Founder

Our Journey

Milestones in craftsmanship and innovation

2010

Sandton Boutique Opening

Launched our flagship boutique in Monte Carlo, introducing our curated approach to luxury retail with just 12 artisan partners.

2013

First Private Collection

Debuted our exclusive private collection featuring limited edition pieces crafted by master jewelers in Paris and Geneva.

2015

Digital Transformation

Pioneered luxury ecommerce with our digital platform, combining cutting-edge technology with white-glove service.

2018

Sustainability Initiative

Committed to ethical sourcing with our Responsible Luxury program, ensuring traceability for all materials.

2022

Global Recognition

Awarded "Best Luxury Ecommerce Experience" by the International Luxury Goods Association.
